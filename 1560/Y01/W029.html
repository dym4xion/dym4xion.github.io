W029: Ingmar Bergman

Week starting: 2021-04-05

Summer with Monkika (1953)

Wild Strawberries (1957)

Persona (1966)

Notes and Conclusions

Not much time lately. They are each very interesting. Summer with Monika deals with new romance and the trouble with getting what you want. Wild Strawberries is an interesting take on the Ivan Ilyich problem - how do you live a good life? and how do you consider life when you are nearing its end? Persona is the most visually arresting but all three are beautifully photographed. I feel the need to reference Lynch and Freud, but it would be wrong to do so as Eraserhead wouldn't come out for another 11 years (so the direction of influence is obvious) and I don't know that much about Freud.
