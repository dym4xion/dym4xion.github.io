W028: David Lean II

Week starting: 2021-03-29

In Which We Serve (1942)

This Happy Breed (1944)

Blithe Spirit (1945)

Notes and Conclusions

They are as much Noel Coward's and Ronald Neame's films as they are David Lean's but Lean's direction is nonetheless punchy and quick witted. As well as this, we see in each of these the two basic features which make a 'Lean' film so good: great characters and a great story. I put each of these very close to (but just short of) Brief Encounter, with This Happy Breed perhaps coming the closest. We're treated here to returning performances by Celia Johnson and John Mills in the first two. All round great fun that I'm sure will stick in my head for a good while.
