Introduction to Database Design and Development

In this unit I explored the fundamental concepts involved in the process designing and developing a database and the business relevancy of databases.

Data Flow Diagram Coursework

For a given case study, we were tasked with developing a level 0 and level 1 data flow diagram to illustrate the data flows, external share holders processes relevant to the case study.

Entity Relationship Diagram Coursework

We continued work based around the case study from the previous coursework by developing and entity relationship diagram for the various entities involved. We needed to consider the cardinality of the relationships as well as ensure the ERD was normalised to the third normal form.

SQL Database Development and Querying Coursework

For a given ERD, series of inital tables and some data, we needed to develop a MySql database and write ten queries that would provide usefull business information.
